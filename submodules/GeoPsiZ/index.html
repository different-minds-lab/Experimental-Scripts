Experiment Summary

In this study, we are interested in modelling how participants like yourself compare different rocks.

This task can be broken down into five sections:

1) a brief survey assessing your level of familiarity with rocks

2) a similarity ranking section where only images of rocks are presented on screen

3) a brief survey on your experience throughout the first similarity ranking section

4) a similarity ranking section where only words are presented on screen

5) a brief survey on your experience throughout the task

Note: Throughout all of these sections, please do not reference any of your course materials.

Trial Format

During each similarity ranking trial, you will be shown 9 images (example shown below). Your task is to select the two images you think are most similar to the center reference image, highlighted in green.

Please select the two images in order of their similarity. That is, first select the image you think is the most similar to the image in the center. Then select the second most similar image to the center image.
